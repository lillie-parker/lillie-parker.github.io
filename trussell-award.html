Student Union Web Map

Trussell Family Scholarship Recipient 2019

After the student union on the University of Oregon campus was renovated, I wanted to explore and display their success rate serving more students with open spaces. I built a small web application with the leaflet library of all interior seating and their access, or lack of access, to wall sockets.

Technology: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Leaflet library

About Me

I am passionate about equity issues and public communication. I believe maps, infographics, and new innovative forms of visual communication can improve public understanding of research and policy.
